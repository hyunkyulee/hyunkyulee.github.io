Research Interest

Research in brain plasticity, cognitive training, and transfer of training. Currently investigating video games as a means to improve perceptual and cognitive abilities in older adults. I am also interested in the effect of individual differences and lifestyle activities in cognitive function and training across the lifespan. Other research interests include visual memory, attention allocation, modification of memory and attention from previous experience.
